About Energy Logan Applications

As a retrofitter, you are always on the field, your team scrambles to put together the information from the audits you perform, and they keep chasing you to get the data, since they see you every two weeks or more. Because of this situation, your proposals and incentive applications are always delayed. Those times are over!

Our first tool launched is Energy Logan Operations. This app solves the retrofitter problems while running the lighting survey. But what happens when the site inspection is done and your calculations are ready to create a proposal? Don't worry, you dont have to do your calculations or hire someone to pretty your proposals up. Energy Logan Sales Portal does it for you!

What is Energy Logan


Energy Logan Tools help you put all the pieces together of your sales and operations processes by introducing the new Sales Portal Application that will allow you to automate closing your lighting deals easier than ever.
Energy Logan Sales Portal works with the information generated by your electricians or lighting consultants in Energy Logan Operations tool, to auto generate beautiful and accurate proposals.
It also allows you to add New Projects and set them in the operations pipeline to be scheduled for a site survey.

The motivation for its development


Being in the energy industry for several years, our team noticed an opportunity to improve and automate processes.
As a retrofitter, chances are that you are working with disparate and ineffective tools like notes, spreadsheets, etc.
The lighting retrofit business, when it comes to expedite incentives, it is known to be beaurocratic with lengthy and tedious processes. In order to fasten the processes you need an automated and accurate process to avoid delays.
This is why, we decided to develop a tool that will help your business to accelarate closing your deals and receiving your incentives faster.
